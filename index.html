My Fav Recipe: Peanut Butter Banana Protein Muffins

Ingredients

  • 3 bananas
  • 1 cup oats
  • 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  • Sugar-free chunky peanut butter
  • Sugar-free chocolate chips
  • Honey
  • Chia seeds

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix everything together in a bowl.
  3. Distribute evenly in muffin tray.
  4. Put in oven for 15 minutes.
  5. Let cool for 5 minutes and enjoy!
